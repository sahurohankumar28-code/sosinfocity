Our Story

About SOS Infotech & Telecom Private Limited

SOS Infotech & Telecom Private Limited was established on 21 March 2016 with a vision to make technology accessible wherever it can improve communication, safety, efficiency, and public services. The company began with a single RF connectivity project aimed at solving real-world communication challenges. Since then, SOS has evolved into a trusted IT and Telecom System Integration company, delivering technology solutions across government, judiciary, railways, healthcare, education, banking, mining, industries, smart cities, and enterprise sectors.

In its early years, SOS specialized in providing reliable last-mile connectivity in remote and challenging locations where conventional networks were unavailable or impractical. The company successfully executed projects for organizations including OSWAN, the Forest Department, the High Court of Odisha, and District Courts. Operating in Odisha, a region frequently affected by cyclones, SOS also played a vital role in maintaining communication for emergency and healthcare services through dependable RF connectivity during critical situations.

As digital transformation accelerated, SOS expanded its expertise beyond connectivity into enterprise networking, campus Wi-Fi, surveillance systems, data centre infrastructure, cybersecurity, cloud-ready networks, command centre integration, and managed IT services. The company was an early adopter of SD-WAN technology, deploying modern network solutions from 2017 and enabling customers to transition from traditional MPLS networks to secure, scalable, and cost-effective architectures by 2021.

A significant milestone came in 2023 when SOS secured major ATMS, ITMS, and IEMS projects for the State Government. Today, the company manages intelligent enforcement, surveillance, secure connectivity, and command centre integration across 250+ critical sites. From a single RF link to complex digital infrastructure projects, SOS continues to build its reputation through innovation, reliability, and execution excellence, with a clear vision of becoming a leading IT and technology solutions company by 2030.
