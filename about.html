Our Story

About SOS Infotech & Telecom Private Limited

SOS Infotech & Telecom Private Limited, established on 21 March 2016, was founded with the vision of making technology accessible to improve communication, safety, efficiency, and public services across sectors. Beginning with a single RF connectivity project, the company has grown into a trusted IT and Telecom System Integration provider serving government, judiciary, railways, healthcare, education, banking, mining, industries, smart cities, and enterprise clients.

In its early years, SOS focused on delivering reliable last-mile connectivity in remote, difficult, and disaster-prone regions, successfully executing projects for organizations such as OSWAN, the Forest Department, the High Court of Odisha, and District Courts, while ensuring uninterrupted communication during emergencies and critical operations.

As digital transformation accelerated, the company expanded its capabilities into enterprise networking, campus Wi-Fi, surveillance systems, data centre infrastructure, cybersecurity, SD-WAN, cloud-ready networks, and managed IT services, helping customers build secure, scalable, and future-ready architectures. Over time, SOS also strengthened its role as a dependable integration partner, combining technical expertise with on-ground execution to support mission-critical environments where reliability is essential.

In 2023, SOS achieved a major milestone by securing ATMS, ITMS, and IEMS projects, and today manages intelligent infrastructure across more than 250 critical sites. From a single RF link to large-scale digital infrastructure, SOS continues to grow with innovation, reliability, and execution excellence, guided by its vision of becoming a leading technology solutions company by 2030.

The company’s journey reflects a strong commitment to solving practical challenges through dependable technology, responsive service, and long-term partnerships that create measurable value for public institutions and private enterprises alike. With a skilled team, a customer-first mindset, and a growing portfolio of integrated solutions, SOS is well positioned to support India’s evolving digital infrastructure needs in the years ahead. Its focus remains on innovation, resilience, and reliable delivery across every project, regardless of scale.
