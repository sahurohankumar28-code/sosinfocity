Our Story

About SOS Infotech & Telecom Private Limited

SOS Infotech & Telecom Private Limited, established on 21 March 2016, began with a vision to make technology accessible for improving communication, safety, operational efficiency, and public services. Starting with a single RF connectivity project, the company has evolved into a trusted IT and Telecom System Integration provider serving government, judiciary, railways, healthcare, education, banking, mining, industries, smart cities, and enterprise customers. Its growth has been driven by a commitment to delivering reliable, scalable, and future-ready technology solutions.

During its early years, SOS specialized in providing dependable last-mile connectivity in remote, disaster-prone, and challenging environments. The company successfully executed critical projects for organizations such as OSWAN, the Forest Department, the High Court of Odisha, and District Courts, ensuring uninterrupted communication for emergency response and mission-critical operations. These projects established SOS as a dependable partner capable of delivering reliable infrastructure under demanding conditions.

As digital transformation accelerated, SOS expanded its expertise into enterprise networking, campus Wi-Fi, surveillance systems, data centre infrastructure, cybersecurity, SD-WAN, cloud-ready networks, and managed IT services. By integrating advanced technologies with strong on-ground execution, the company has enabled organizations to build secure, resilient, and high-performance digital ecosystems. In 2023, SOS achieved a significant milestone by securing ATMS, ITMS, and IEMS projects, and today manages intelligent infrastructure across more than 250 critical sites.

From its beginnings with a single RF link to managing large-scale digital infrastructure, SOS continues to grow through innovation, technical excellence, and customer-focused execution. Guided by its vision of becoming a leading technology solutions company by 2030, the organization remains committed to delivering dependable solutions, fostering long-term partnerships, and supporting India's rapidly evolving digital infrastructure across both public and private sectors.
