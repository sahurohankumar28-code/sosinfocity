Our Story

About SOS Infotech & Telecom Private Limited

SOS Infotech & Telecom Private Limited was founded on 21st March 2016 with a belief that technology should reach every place where progress, safety, service, and communication matter.

Our journey began with a single RF connectivity project. It was a modest beginning, but it carried a strong purpose: to solve real connectivity and technology challenges on the ground. From that first step, SOS has grown steadily into a full-scale IT and Telecom System Integration company, delivering critical digital infrastructure across government, judiciary, railways, healthcare, education, banking, mining, industries, smart city, and enterprise sectors.

In our early years, we built our strength through last-mile connectivity, especially in difficult and remote locations where conventional networks were either unavailable, unreliable, or commercially unviable. We delivered critical connectivity projects for institutions and departments such as OSWAN, Forest Department, High Court of Odisha, and District Courts.

Odisha is a coastal state and often faces cyclones and natural challenges. During such difficult times, SOS has supported critical medical and emergency services through reliable RF connectivity, helping communication continue when it was needed the most. These experiences shaped our character as a company — practical, committed, responsible, and ready to perform under pressure.

As industries and institutions started moving towards digital transformation, SOS also evolved. We expanded from connectivity into enterprise networking, secured surveillance, campus Wi-Fi, data centre infrastructure, cloud-ready networks, cybersecurity, smart monitoring, command centre integration, and managed IT services.

In 2017, when many organizations were still dependent on expensive traditional MPLS networks, SOS started adopting and deploying SD-WAN connectivity solutions. By 2021, we had helped many of our customers move towards more flexible, secure, and cost-effective SD-WAN-based network architecture.

Our journey took a major leap in 2023, when SOS secured significant ATMS, ITMS, and IEMS projects for State Government requirements. Since then, we have developed rich experience in delivering intelligent enforcement, smart surveillance, secured connectivity, and command centre-linked digital infrastructure across 250+ critical enforcement sites.

Today, SOS is no longer only a telecom connectivity company. We are a growing technology organization with capabilities across IT infrastructure, telecom, network security, surveillance, automation, smart mobility, digital monitoring, data centre solutions, and emerging technology integration.

From a single RF link to complex multi-location digital infrastructure, our story is built on trust, execution, learning, and continuous growth. Every project has made us stronger. Every challenge has made us sharper. Every customer has helped us move closer to our larger goal — to become an industry-leading IT company by 2030.

Purpose & Direction

What Drives Us Forward

Our Mission

  • Our mission is to deliver reliable, secure, scalable, and future-ready technology solutions that help organizations stay connected, protected, intelligent, and digitally enabled.
  • We aim to create practical and high-performance IT and telecom infrastructure for government departments, enterprises, industries, institutions, and critical service providers. Our focus is not only on deploying technology, but on ensuring that the technology performs in real-world conditions.
  • We are committed to building solutions in areas such as enterprise networking, RF and fibre connectivity, SD-WAN, campus Wi-Fi, large-scale surveillance, smart city systems, data centres, cloud-ready infrastructure, cybersecurity, command centres, automation, and AI-enabled digital platforms.
  • Our mission is to support our customers through complete lifecycle services planning, designing, supply, installation, integration, commissioning, monitoring, support, and continuous improvement.
  • At SOS, we believe technology must create value. It must improve uptime, security, visibility, decision-making, public safety, service delivery, and operational efficiency.

Our Vision

  • Our vision is to drive SOS Infotech & Telecom Private Limited towards becoming an industry-leading IT and Telecom System Integration company by 2030.
  • We aspire to build a future-ready organization with strong technical capability, modern infrastructure expertise, trusted partnerships, skilled manpower, and the ability to deliver large-scale digital transformation projects across India.
  • By 2030, our goal is to position SOS as a recognized name in smart infrastructure, secured connectivity, enterprise IT, surveillance intelligence, data centre solutions, automation, cloud-enabled networks, AI-based monitoring, and integrated digital platforms.
  • We want to grow beyond traditional system integration and become a technology partner that helps governments, enterprises, industries, and institutions build smarter, safer, and more connected ecosystems.
