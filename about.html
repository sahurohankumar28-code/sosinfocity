Our Story

Engineering resilience since 2016

Our company has grown steadily since its inception seven years ago. We specialize in wireless networks, CCTV security installations, and advanced communication services across Assam, Odisha, Jharkhand, Bihar, and other parts of North Eastern and South Eastern India. We are also equipped to deliver our services nationwide.

We handle projects of all sizes, from small setups to large-scale deployments, covering wireless LAN & WAN solutions, CCTV installations, biometrics, optical solutions, and next-gen security systems. In addition to infrastructure, we provide website design and development services, proudly contributing to building Bhubaneswar as a leading smart city with seamless wireless connectivity.

What drives us forward

Our Mission

Backed by quality and driven by expertise, SOS focuses on delivering complete customer satisfaction. Our growth is rooted in understanding client requirements and providing efficient, reliable solutions. Our strong after-sales support and commitment to service excellence have earned us the trust of our clients. We believe we play an important role in contributing to India's vision of becoming a global digital powerhouse.

Our Vision

Our vision is to deliver top-quality services to all clients at an affordable cost, ensuring timely execution without compromising standards. We aim to become the most trusted partner for intelligent infrastructure and cyber security across India, enabling enterprises to thrive in a connected digital era with cutting-edge, sustainable solutions.
