Deterministic Processing, Powered Locally

In modern industrial environments, waiting for telemetry vectors to make a round trip to centralized cloud facilities introduces a systematic buffer penalty. For processes operating at sub-millisecond tolerances, this network payload lag isn't just inefficient—it can compromise operational integrity.

To balance operations, enterprise plants run a cyclical three-part pipeline close to the mechanical hardware: data ingestion, state evaluation, and systemic control adjustments. Localized gateways handle mission-critical anomalies independently of WAN stability.
