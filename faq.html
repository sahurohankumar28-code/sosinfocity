We've Got Answers

Everything you need to know about working with SOS Infocity — from implementation to ongoing support.

What types of organizations does SOS work with?

We work with government bodies, educational institutions, industrial organizations, NGOs, and enterprise clients across different sectors and locations nationwide.

Can SOS handle projects across multiple cities or sites?

Yes. Our teams are experienced in delivering and supporting projects across campuses, districts, and multi-location setups with centralized management.

Do your solutions work with existing systems?

Our solutions are designed to integrate seamlessly with current infrastructure wherever possible, ensuring operations continue without major disruptions.

Does SOS provide support after project completion?

Yes. We offer comprehensive ongoing technical support, system monitoring, and maintenance services based on your specific project requirements.

What is your typical response time for support requests?

We offer 24/7 global support with response times ranging from 1-4 hours depending on severity level. Critical issues are addressed immediately.

How does SOS ensure data security and compliance?

We implement zero-trust architecture, end-to-end encryption, regular security audits, and comply with industry standards like ISO 27001, GDPR, and local regulations.

Do you offer cybersecurity training for employees?

Yes, we provide comprehensive security awareness training programs to help your team identify threats and follow best practices.

Can projects be customized for specific operational needs?

Absolutely. Each deployment is planned according to site conditions, usage requirements, and long-term operational goals for maximum efficiency.

How do we start a discussion with the SOS team?

You can connect through the contact form, email us at contact@sosinfocity.com, or call our helpline. Our team will review your requirements and guide the next steps.

What is the typical timeline for project implementation?

Timelines vary based on project scope and complexity. After initial consultation, we provide a detailed timeline with milestones and deliverables.

Do you offer surveillance solutions for existing buildings?

Yes, we provide retrofitting solutions for existing infrastructure, including IP cameras, access control systems, and centralized monitoring platforms.

Is remote support available for all solutions?

Most of our solutions include remote monitoring and support capabilities. On-site support is also available when required for hardware or physical infrastructure.
